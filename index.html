How to Use a Camera

An Instruction Challenge for JOUR108

Taking great photos can feel difficult at first, but breaking it down makes it easy.

Time to read: 5 minutes
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

  • Things You'll Need
  • Camera
  • SD Card
  • Patience
  • Optional: Editing Software

  1. Familiarize Yourself With Your Camera

    Learn your camera’s buttons and settings using the manual or quick start guide.

  2. Insert and Format Your SD Card

    Insert your SD card and format it in settings to prepare it for use.

    Results you can get from good shooting practice.

  3. Adjust Camera Settings

    Learn ISO, aperture, and shutter speed (the exposure triangle) and experiment.

  4. Experiment

    Practice is the key to improving photography skills.

  5. Optional: Edit Photos

    Editing helps refine your images. Shooting in RAW gives more flexibility.
